Production Tips

🎬 TikTok Filming

Film vertically. Good lighting (face a window). Large text on screen. Add captions. Post 6-9 PM EAT. First 2 seconds must hook.

📱 Instagram Stories

Use native stickers (polls, questions, countdowns) — Instagram boosts them. Mix video + static. Reply to poll results next day. Use location tag "Tanzania".

⏰ Batch Production

Film all 10 TikToks in 2-3 sessions. Change your shirt between takes. Create all poster designs in one sitting. Schedule IG posts via Meta Business Suite.

📊 Best Posting Times

Tanzania audience: 6-9 PM EAT weekdays, 10 AM-12 PM weekends. TikTok: post daily. Instagram feed: 3-4x/week. Stories: 2-3 per day.
